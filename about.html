About me

I did my first skydive in June 1994. Since then, I've logged numerous skydives with wonderful people, in beautiful places, and during exciting events and competitions.

My main fire of emotion in skydiving has always been freefall photography, but I also held various instructor ratings for two decades.

In my opinion, serious camera flying is one of the most demanding disciplines in skydiving. Therefore, nowadays, I focus more on just-for-fun jumping with friends and other toys instead of camera flying, as the number of my annual skydives has been increasing.
